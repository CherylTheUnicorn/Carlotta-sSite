Curriculum

I've attended the Primary School for five years and then the Junior High School for three, they both were located in my small village.

After those eight years, I went in a bigger town and attended a School of Languages (the so-called Liceo Linguistico).
Here I had the possibility to learn English, German and French.
It was during this period that I attended many courses focused on the English and German languages.

I got the Cambridge English Advanced (CAE- C1) Qualification and the Goethe Institut Certificate (A2).
I graduated in 2017 and I moved to Milan in September in order to study Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano (PoliMi).
